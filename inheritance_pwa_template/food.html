📋 Halal Food Guidelines

✅ What is Halal:

  • • Meat from animals slaughtered according to Islamic law
  • • Fish and seafood (all types)
  • • Fruits, vegetables, and grains
  • • Dairy products from halal animals
  • • Eggs from halal birds

❌ What is Haram:

  • • Pork and pork products
  • • Alcohol and intoxicants
  • • Meat not slaughtered properly
  • • Carnivorous animals
  • • Blood and blood products
