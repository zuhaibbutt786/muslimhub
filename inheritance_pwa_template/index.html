📖 Allah's Orders About Inheritance

🕌 Quranic Verses on Inheritance

Surah An-Nisa (4:11)

Allah commands you concerning your children:

يُوصِيكُمُ اللَّهُ فِي أَوْلَادِكُمْ ۖ لِلذَّكَرِ مِثْلُ حَظِّ الْأُنثَيَيْنِ

"Allah commands you concerning your children: for the male, what is equal to the share of two females."

Surah An-Nisa (4:12)

Allah commands you concerning spouses and parents:

وَلَكُمْ نِصْفُ مَا تَرَكَ أَزْوَاجُكُمْ إِن لَّمْ يَكُن لَّهُنَّ وَلَدٌ

"And for you is half of what your wives leave if they have no child."

📚 Hadith on Inheritance

Narrated by Abu Huraira (RA)

The Prophet ﷺ said:

"Learn the laws of inheritance and teach them, for they are half of knowledge."

- Sahih Muslim

⚖️ Key Islamic Inheritance Principles

📋

Fixed Shares

Allah has prescribed specific shares for each family member in the Quran. These shares are fixed and cannot be altered.

⚖️

Justice & Fairness

Islamic inheritance ensures fair distribution according to Allah's wisdom, considering family relationships and responsibilities.

🔄

Priority System

There's a specific order of priority: spouse, parents, children, then extended family members.

📊

Mathematical Precision

Each share is calculated precisely to ensure the total distribution equals 100% of the estate.
