How we protect your data and maintain your privacy

Last Updated: January 2024

Introduction

At Islamic Inheritance Calculator, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our inheritance calculator.

  • Estate values and asset information you enter
  • Family member details for inheritance calculations
  • Calculation results and distribution data

Note: This data is processed locally in your browser and is not stored on our servers.

🌐 Usage Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Language preferences
  • Pages visited and time spent

📧 Contact Information

  • Name and email address (only when you contact us)
  • Message content and subject
  • Contact form submissions

How We Use Your Information

🎯 Primary Purposes

  • Provide inheritance calculations based on Islamic law
  • Display live gold and silver rates
  • Improve calculator functionality and accuracy

📈 Analytics & Improvement

  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Ensure website performance and security
